What is SSL?

SSL can be used by businesses or individuals to decrease the risk of sensitive information being stolen or tampered with by hackers and identity thieves (credit card numbers, usernames, passwords, emails, etc.)

When you see a padlock icon next to the URL in the address bar, that means SSL protects the website you are visiting.
